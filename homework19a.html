Notes
  1. The pronoun for he/she/it is यह or वह depending on whether you are trying to say this thing/person/… or that thing/person/….
  2. For his/her/its, use इसका when you mean this person's or this thing's. If you mean that person's or that thing's, use the word उसका. The words इनका and उनका are used similarly to mean their.
  3. The ending for the genitive or possessive cases are exactly the same as the endings for adjectives that we did in the last class. The ending depends on the noun that is being talked about, NOT on the person who it belongs to. For instance, my dog will always be मेरा कुत्ता no matter if a girl or boy is saying it.
