Matras

The ि matra (the i sound in pick)

हि hi यि yi मि mi वि vi रि ri नि ni कि ki

The ा matra (the a sound in father)

हा ha या ya मा ma वा va रा ra ना na का ka

The े matra (the ay sound in pay)

हे hay ये yay मे may वे vay रे ray ने nay के kay

The ी matra (the ee sound in peek)

ही hee यी yee मी mee वी vee री ree नी nee की kee
