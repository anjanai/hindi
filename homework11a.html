नाम: --------------------------------------------------

The oddball र (the U and OO matras, and the half R): रु, रू, कर्म,

Points to remember about the HALF R

  1. To write a word like VARMAA which is VA + R + MAA, you first write VA + MAA (वमा); then since the the R comes AFTER VA, you put the half R AFTER व - on top of मा = वर्मा
  2. To know when you need a HALF R, think of the word in English. If the R is followed by a consonant, then it is a HALF R. In the word garmee गर्मी, the r is followed by m - so we need a HALF R. But in a word like garam गरम, the r is followed by a vowel, so we need a full R.
  3. To read a letter combination like र्मा, you read the R first, and then the MAA - so that would be RMAA
  4. There are no words in Hindi that begin with a half R, so you will never see the half R on top of the first letter.
  5. Since the half R, occupies the position on top of the line, it has to share space with the ि, ी, े etc. matras. In all of these cases, the half R sign occurs after the matra like this : र्मि र्मी र्मे र्मो
