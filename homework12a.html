नाम: --------------------------------------------------

More on the oddball र: half letter + full R

Points to remember:

  1. Note the difference between:
    (Full letter +) half R (+ full letter): कर्म (KA + R + MA)
    and
    Half letter + full R : क्रम (K + RA + MA)
  2. You can think of how you would write the word in English. If a consonant is followed by another consonant, the first consonant has to be HALF. In KRAM: K is half, R is full. In KARM: K is full, R is half. The ending letter in Hindi is usually a full letter).
  3. To write Half letter + full R, here are the rules:
    1. If the letter has a line at the bottom, add a / at the left of the line. Examples: क्र ग्र च्र ज्र न्र
    2. If not, add a ^ at the bottom. Examples: ट्र ड्र ढ्र छ्र
    3. There are a couple of exceptions: one is half त + र is usually written as त्र. The other is a letter we haven't learned yet - and we will go over that later.
